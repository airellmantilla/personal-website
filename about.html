Hi, my name is Airell Mantilla and I am a first generation Filipino-Canadian.

I'm currently studying Computer Science at Seneca College, which is something I've been passionate about for a while now. Before coming to Seneca, I attended Georgian College for Graphic Design, but unfortunately, I ended up dropping out after a year. After that, I decided to pursue a different path and enrolled in Police Foundations at Humber College, which I successfully graduated from with honours.

Although my journey to finding my passion has had its ups and downs, I'm grateful for the experiences that have led me to where I am today.
